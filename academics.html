Coursers Offered

RGUKT offers a six-year integrated course after the 10th class examination leading to a B.Tech. degree, and aim to provide core concepts and skills in the broad areas of engineering, sciences, mathematics, and humanities.

Pre University Course

The first part of the six-year course is a two-year Pre University Course (equivalent to (AP) Intermediate) . The pre-university offers premature specialization in tracks such as Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Life sciences (equivalent to MPC + BiPC). By bringing students into the University at an earlier stage we hope to impart a broad-based education including humanities and liberal arts to students. Visit Website.

Computer Science and Engineering

A course that studies practical and theoretical knowledge of computer hardware and software. Students are given the strong foundation in the field of IT and Computer science. Many workshops are specially conducted in every semester which enable students to explore wider application of IT and Computer Science. The department is committed to the overall development of its students. Visit Website.

Electrical & Communication Engineering

A course that establishes a unique learning environment to enable the students to face the challenges of the Electronics and Communication Engineering field. Students are introduced to core courses that include as well as the programming. Electronics Workshops are conducted that are intended to give students hands-on experience in electronics and various technologies.Visit Website

Mechanical Engineering

A course that transforms students into outstanding mechanical engineers with strong domain knowledge and skills. The Mechanical Engineering field of study includes technical subject areas such as thermal engineering, design, manufacturing, materials sciences, CAD etc. To impart training to students many workshops are conducted to promote the establishment of centre of excellence in technology areas.Visit Website

Civil Engineering

A course that prepares the students to design and construct structures with a focus on creativity and innovation, and a strong ethical responsibility. Students are supported with well- developed curriculum and syllabus to meet contemporary industrial needs and equip the students to meet future market demand. To foster the spirit of innovation and creativity among students, many workshops are conducted.Visit Website
